👋 Our Story

Built by Designers, For Designers

We believe professional CAD tools should be accessible to everyone — not locked behind expensive software licenses or complex installations.

🎯 Our Mission

Democratizing CAD Design

AutoCAD Web Studio was born out of frustration with the status quo. Why should a student learning mechanical design, a freelance architect, or a startup engineer have to spend hundreds of dollars on software just to start creating?

We built a browser-based CAD platform that gives you the tools you need — 2D drafting, 3D modeling, dimension tools, layers, and more — completely free, with no download required.

Our platform is inspired by industry-standard AutoCAD workflows so that anything you learn here transfers directly to professional environments.

What We Stand For

These core values guide every decision we make.

🆓

Free Forever

Core tools will always be free. We believe access to professional design software shouldn't be a privilege of those who can afford expensive licenses.

⚡

Zero Friction

No downloads, no accounts required to start. Open a browser, open the canvas, start drawing. We optimize for the fastest path from idea to design.

🎓

Education First

We use AutoCAD-standard shortcuts and workflows so students and professionals can learn transferable skills that apply everywhere in the industry.

🔒

Privacy Focused

Your designs stay on your device. We don't store your drawings or sell your data. What you create is yours, always.

🌍

Globally Accessible

Designed to work on any device, anywhere in the world — with offline capability so you can keep working even without internet.

🚀

Constantly Improving

We ship updates every week based on user feedback. If you're missing a tool or feature, let us know — we build what you actually need.

Our Journey

From a weekend project to a platform trusted by thousands.

January 2022

The Beginning

Alex builds the first prototype — a simple HTML5 canvas with line and circle tools — in a weekend hackathon. Shares it on Twitter and gets 2,000 stars overnight.

June 2022

Team Grows

Priya and Marcus join the project. The team adds layer management, dimension tools, and the first version of undo/redo history. User base reaches 10,000.

February 2023

3D Studio Launch

Marcus ships the Three.js-powered 3D studio. First day sees 5,000 users creating 3D models. Featured in multiple design newsletters and blogs.

October 2023

100K Users

Platform reaches 100,000 registered users across 45 countries. Sofia joins to lead community growth. Full shortcut reference library added.

2024 — Now

Continuous Growth

New tools ship weekly. Community feature requests drive roadmap. Plans for collaboration features, DWG import/export, and mobile apps in progress.
